Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Forma.

How accurate is the AI recognition?

Our AI is trained on millions of food images and achieves 95%+ accuracy for common meals. For complex dishes, it uses advanced computer vision to identify individual ingredients and portions. You can always adjust the results if needed.

Does it work with restaurant meals?

Absolutely! Forma excels at recognizing restaurant dishes, even complex plated meals. Simply snap a photo or describe what you ordered, and our AI will estimate the calories and macros based on typical restaurant portions.

Is my data private and secure?

Yes. Your data is encrypted end-to-end and stored securely. We never sell your information to third parties, and you can delete your data at any time. We're GDPR compliant and take privacy seriously.

What platforms are supported?

Forma is available on both iOS (iPhone/iPad) and Android devices. You can download the beta version right now and start tracking immediately. A web version is coming soon.
