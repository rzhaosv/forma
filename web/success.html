Quick Start Guide

Get the Most Out of Forma

Follow these simple principles to start seeing results with AI tracking today.

1. Snap Before You Eat

The best time to log is right when your plate arrives. Snap a photo in 2 seconds and enjoy your meal knowing your macros are already being calculated.

2. Voice Log on the Go

Driving or walking? Just say "I had a protein bar and a coffee." Forma's AI understands natural language and logs it instantly.

3. Use Barcode for Snacks

For packaged foods, the barcode scanner is the fastest way to get 100% accurate data from our database of millions of products.
