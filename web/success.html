Busy Professional's Guide to Macros

Stop guessing. Here is the cheat sheet to hitting your goals without spending hours in the kitchen.

1. The 30/30/30 Rule

For sustainable energy, aim for 30g of protein at breakfast, within 30 minutes of waking up. This stabilizes blood sugar and reduces cravings later in the day.

2. Protein is King

If you track nothing else, track protein. Aim for 0.7g - 1g per pound of body weight. It keeps you full and builds muscle, which burns more fat at rest.

3. Don't Drink Your Calories

Latte: 300kcal. Soda: 150kcal. Juice: 200kcal. Swap these for water, black coffee, or tea, and you can save 500+ calories a day effortlessly.
