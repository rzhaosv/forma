## ğŸ¨ Brand Voice Guidelines

### Personality
- **Supportive, not preachy** - We're your friend, not your drill sergeant
- **Smart, not complicated** - We explain the "why" without jargon
- **Motivating, not guilt-tripping** - Focus on wins, not failures
- **Human, not corporate** - Write like you're texting a friend

### Dos âœ…
- Use "you" and "your" (direct address)
- Keep sentences short and punchy
- Use emojis sparingly but effectively
- Focus on benefits, not features
- Tell stories and use examples
- Address objections head-on
- Be honest about limitations

### Don'ts âŒ
- Don't use diet culture language ("cheat days," "guilty pleasure")
- Don't shame users for missing days or going over calories
- Don't make unrealistic promises ("lose 30 lbs in 30 days!")
- Don't use corporate jargon or buzzwords
- Don't be overly technical about AI/ML
- Don't compare to competitors by name
- Don't pressure users to upgrade constantly

## ğŸ† Conversion Optimization Tips

### Paywall Best Practices

1. **Show the paywall early** - Day 1 or after first scan limit
2. **Use free trial** - 7 days removes purchase friction
3. **Highlight annual savings** - "$60/year savings!" is powerful
4. **Add urgency** - "Trial expires in 6:23:45"
5. **Social proof** - "10,000+ premium users"
6. **Money-back guarantee** - Removes risk
7. **One-tap restore** - For previous subscribers

### Copy Optimization Principles

1. **Benefits > Features** - "Track faster" not "AI-powered scanning"
2. **Concrete > Abstract** - "10x faster" not "much faster"
3. **Social Proof** - Numbers, testimonials, ratings
4. **Objection Handling** - Address "too expensive," "don't need it," "not sure"
5. **Scarcity** - "Trial ends soon" (but be honest)
6. **Urgency** - "Start today" (but don't be pushy)
7. **Clarity** - Short sentences, simple words

### Subscription Pricing Psychology

1. **Annual Discounts** - 50% off shows huge value
2. **Monthly Price on Annual** - "$5/mo" instead of "$59.99/year"
3. **Price Anchoring** - Compare to nutrition coaching ($200/mo)
4. **Free Trial** - Lowers barrier, increases conversions
5. **Default to Annual** - Pre-select best value option
6. **Show Savings** - "Save $60" in big text
