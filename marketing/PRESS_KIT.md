## 3. The Story (Founder Bio)

**Ray Zhao** built Forma out of personal frustration. As a busy professional, he found that traditional "manual entry" apps were the first things he dropped when work got intense. He realized that for a health tool to be effective for people like him, it had to be **invisible**.

"I wanted an app that felt like a nutritionist looking over my shoulder, not a spreadsheet I had to manage," Ray explains. After months of training AI models to understand portion sizes and meal composition, Forma was born.
